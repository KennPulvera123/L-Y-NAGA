Our Programs & Services 🎯

We offer Early Intervention and Developmental Programs along with Special Learning Programs through consultation with our multi-disciplinary team of specialists

✨ ⭐

Speech Therapy Program 🗣️

The primary objective is to correct articulation disorders, voice disorders, and language delay. Activities include vocal practice, structured conversation, story telling, dramatization, and Q&A sessions.

• Articulation disorder correction • Voice disorder treatment • Language delay remediation
🗨️ 💬

Occupational Therapy Program 🖐️

Assessment and treatment using specific, purposeful activities to prevent disability and promote independent function. Helps children achieve age-appropriate self-help, play, and learning skills.

• Age-appropriate self-help skills • Play and learning development • Independent function promotion
🧠 💡

Behavioral Therapy 🧠

Professional behavioral intervention helping children develop positive behaviors, social skills, and emotional regulation strategies in a supportive and structured environment.

• Positive behavior development • Social skills training • Emotional regulation support
🎓 📚

Early Intervention Program 📚

Comprehensive early intervention services for infants and toddlers with developmental delays or disabilities. Focuses on supporting families and enhancing children's development during critical early years.

• Family-centered early intervention • Developmental milestone support • Transition planning services

Professional Consultation Services 👨‍⚕️

Our programs are delivered through consultation with a multi-disciplinary team of medical professionals and specialists

🩺 ⭐

Developmental Pediatrician 👶

Provides medical services to infants, children, and adolescents. Trained in overall growth and development, they are often the first point of contact for developmental concerns.

🖐️ 💫

Occupational Therapist 🖐️

Provides assessment and treatment using specific, purposeful activities to prevent disability and promote independent function in all aspects of daily life.

👩‍🏫 📚

SPED Teachers 👩‍🏫

Provide specialized instruction using remedial techniques, individualized education programs (IEP), and early identification support to help children with specific learning disabilities.

🗣️ 💬

Speech and Language Pathologist 🗣️

Specializes in communication disorders, providing assessment and treatment for speech articulation, language development, and communication skills enhancement.

About Lance Yuri Kids SPOT Center 💝

Lance Yuri Kids SPOT Center is a pediatric center for children with special needs. Our goals are: To provide a quality therapy service that assists the child, as a whole, to achieve the highest level of skills possible within the scope of their cognitive and physical function. To educate and inform the adults involved with these children to improved understanding and acceptance of their unique abilities and challenges.
